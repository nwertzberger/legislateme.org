Legislate Me

This will be a site dedicated to informing the United States about everything legislature is doing. This information is already available, but the need for a law degree to understand it seems to undermine the purpose of these being public. In an effort to stop the media, who is completely partisan to either the Republican or Democratic Party, from having total control over what the general public understands about what is happening in the world. This site will cut through the bullshit and offer a clean interface showing current bills, who started them, and who supports them.

In other words, this site is only concerned with action, not rhetoric. This site also does not forget.
